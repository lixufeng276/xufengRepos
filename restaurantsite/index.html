Restaurant Description:
Cafe by day, Restaurant by night. We are an animal-friendly cafe located in Dumbo that outlooks the East River. Enjoy the great view of the city's skyline and a few of the city's suspension bridges connecting Brooklyn to Manhattan, but most importantly, enjoy our all American cuisine! Wifi is provide while you sit in to enjoy our menu. A nice and comfy study space for getting those brain juices flowing.

Come in by 4PM and enjoy some live music played by local artists. We host trivia night every Sunday at 7pm!
